Legero Bright Future is driven by a profound belief in the transformative power of light. Our vision is rooted in the understanding that the significance of proper lighting cannot be overstated. Our core commitment is to provide lighting solutions that seamlessly align with the unique architecture and design of each space.

View live site

Create an intuitive and visually attractive website for Legero International. Showcase the variety and quality of their product offerings. Facilitate easy navigation and access to company information, store locations, dealer networks, and customer support. Enable smooth career applications and partner inquiries. Increase engagement through blog posts and media content.

The website is designed to offer a seamless and engaging user experience. The About Page provides an overview of Legero’s history, leadership, and values, focusing on their commitment to sustainability and innovation, with images of their process and community impact. The Product Page showcases a wide range of decorative lights, from modern fixtures to classic designs, with features like an interactive gallery, high-resolution images and detailed descriptions. Customers can filter products by category, check real-time stock availability and read reviews. The Store Locator Page helps users find nearby Legero stores and authorized dealers via a dynamic map and location filters, with store details and Google Maps integration for easy navigation.

The Blog Page offers regular content on lighting trends, décor tips, and industry insights, with guest blogs and a featured post section, encouraging social media sharing. On the Careers Page, job seekers can explore open positions, learn about the company culture, and submit resumes via an online form, while employee testimonials highlight the workplace environment. The Contact Us Page centralizes inquiries, offering categorized contact forms, phone numbers, and email addresses, along with a global office map and links to social media for instant interaction.

The Media Page highlights Legero's media appearances, product launches, and press releases, with downloadable press kits, embedded videos, and links to articles. The Dealership Page invites potential partners to inquire about dealership opportunities through a form and provides information on partnership benefits, success stories, and expansion strategies. Finally, the Support Page offers comprehensive customer assistance with an FAQ section, live chat support, downloadable product guides, and a ticket submission feature for more specific inquiries.
The key results from the website revamp include a Increased engagement was evident as the blog and media pages saw a notable rise in traffic, with users spending more time exploring expert insights and company news. Improved user experience was achieved through the redesigned product pages and store locator, providing a seamless journey from product discovery to purchase. The dealership page successfully attracted new business partners, resulting in a 20% increase in dealer inquiries within the first six months. Additionally, the streamlined support system reduced customer service workload by 35%, thanks to a robust FAQ and knowledge base, which minimized repetitive queries
