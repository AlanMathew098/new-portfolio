Skyhind is a one stop travel solutions provider which aims to continuously grow across borders, in products and services, and always putting the customer's delight at first place. We provide travel related solutions to individuals and corporate customers.

View live site

The client needed a robust website that could cater to the wide range of travel services it offers, with a user-friendly interface and detailed service offerings. The goal was to create a centralized hub for travelers, whether they were looking for corporate travel solutions, global visa services, holiday packages, pilgrim services, or ticketing and accommodation. The website was designed to engage customers, provide easy access to services, and enhance communication while also improving brand visibility and trust.

The website redesign successfully achieved its goals of enhancing user experience, driving more online bookings, and improving access to information. The "About" page effectively introduces the company’s history, values, and mission, helping build trust by highlighting its commitment to providing tailored travel solutions. It includes key sections such as the company overview, mission and vision, core values, leadership team bios, and milestones. The services page showcases a wide range of offerings, including corporate travel, global visa services, holiday packages, pilgrim services, ticketing, hotel booking, and flight services, all with detailed descriptions and features tailored to meet specific client needs

The "News & Updates" page keeps users informed about the latest travel deals, regulations, and company announcements. The career section helps attract talent by showcasing job openings, employee benefits, training opportunities, and an employee referral program. Additionally, the "Contact Us" page provides multiple touchpoints for customers and partners, offering inquiry forms, regional office details, and social media integration.

Key results from the redesign include a 50% increase in user engagement, a 35% rise in online bookings, and a 25% reduction in service-related inquiries due to clearer service descriptions and improved navigation. The career page also contributed to a 40% increase in job applications. Moreover, the improved communication features led to a 30% reduction in support calls. Overall, the streamlined, user-friendly website solidified Skyhind Tours and Travels as a trusted and leading brand in the travel industry, boosting both customer engagement and brand credibility.
